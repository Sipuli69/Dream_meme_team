BIOGRAPHY


BlanKoliver is the freshest and hardest hitting rap artist straight from downtown Hämptön, Finland.

During his impressive career he has left his mark in the rap history thanks to a combination of unique craftsmanship and undying source of the most genius lyrics. He has been conquering the universe since the Christmas Eve of 2020 and is already at the top with other such legends as Lil B, PaRappa the Rapper, this kid and many others.

His biggest influences in music are Gettomasa, Puukko-Allu, Teräsbetoni and Michelangelo from Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

His music touches the dark subjects of heartbreak and the toughness of his earlier years on the streets of Finnish Chicago; Lahti, as well as many other very relatable life altering horrible events such as Sanna Marin becoming the prime minister of Finland.

On 22th of January 2021 BlanKoliver released his new single "Chicago", which was his best song to date (and the only one at the same time).

BlanKoliver carries a gun everywhere he goes, because "he can't leave his second genital anywhere".
